French

At Mass. Academy, we can either take French or Spanish as a foreign language. I chose French, and have been learning French since seventh grade. In class, our teacher, Mme. Wildfong, encourages us to step outside our comfort zone and really try to get a good grasp of the language. In this class, it is not how much someone knows that is measured, but how much they improve that really matters. So far, we have watched several films in French and have also read multiple texts, such as Beauty and the Beast, in French.

In French, we had to write a fairy tale after reading Beauty and the Beast. This fairy tale had to be completely original, and include the elements of a good fairy tale which include magic, nature, magical objects, etc. I wrote a love story about a man who loves a woman and finally proposes to her. But then, tragedy strikes the lovers. Read the fairy tale to find out what happens next. Oh, and you might need to know French to do that.

In French, we also had a French food day, where everyone in the class picked a French dish and made it. Later, for lunch one day, we all ate the dishes that the class had prepared. They were all delicious! We also had to give a presentation about our dish to the class in French about what we made, the ingredients, and how we made it. For my dish, I made French cream puffs.
