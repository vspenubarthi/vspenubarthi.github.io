Science and Technical Writing

To ensure that all the students in the school are prepared for academic success, Mass. Academy has a Scientific and Technical Writing (STW) course as well. In this course, we are instructed in how to properly write scientific writings and communicate our results with other professionals. In addition, we also review standard English conventions and write our thesis for our independent STEM projects as well.

In STW, we read many scientific articles and analyze them. For this assignment, we were allowed to choose an article from a field of interest and analyze it. I love the field of artificial intelligence and love learning about its real-world applications, so I analyzed an article which discussed the use of artificial intelligence in weather prediction, and the idea that artificial intelligence can be used to create a comprehensive weather model by taking the best features of existing weather models.

We also summarized a paper related to our STEM project in STW to continue to develop our skills in scientific writing. This summary focuses on a paper I read in which the authors attempted to increase the performances of geofences by implementing a new geofencing technique which primarily uses a global navigation satellite system. I also worked on summarizing the procedure in this paper as well as creating a table representing all the scenarios tested.
